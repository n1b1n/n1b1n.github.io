The Vision

Amal Suresh is a concept artist focused on the intersection of scale and emotion. Based in Kerala, India, he creates digital paintings that serve as blueprints for games and film.

Every piece is a study of light, color, and narrative, designed to immerse the viewer in a world that doesn't yet exist.
